Smallest Number/Circle Game

repl.it This experiment was created by students Juan, Ken, and Lucas in SENG 411 at the University of Victoria. The player is going to go through 18 trials. The first 9 trials, the player has to click the smallest number on screen. The last 9 trials, the player has to click the smallest circle on screen. The game will record the player's accuracy and speed during each of the trials.
